VoxTrim Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Our commitment: VoxTrim is a fully offline audio recording and editing app. Your recordings are stored only on your local device and are never uploaded to any server.

1. Information We Access

VoxTrim respects your privacy. The app accesses the following data:

  • Microphone: Used only when you actively record. Audio data is saved locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server.
  • Storage: Used only to save and read your recording files. No other files are accessed.
  • Speech-to-Text: Uses a local AI model (Whisper) for transcription. Audio data never leaves your device.

2. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not upload recording data
  • We do not track user behavior
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not make any network requests

3. Data Storage

All recordings and project data are stored locally on your device:

  • Project files: App-private directory
  • Exported files: A directory of your choice (e.g., the Music folder)

You can delete recordings at any time through the in-app project manager, or clear all app data through system settings.

4. Permissions

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Core functionality for audio recording. Only active when you tap the record button.
  • Storage access: Used to export recordings to public directories (e.g., Music folder).

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Policy Changes

If this policy is updated, we will notify you upon app update and request your consent again.
